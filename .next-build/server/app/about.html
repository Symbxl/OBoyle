About

Caring for Bloomfield properties since 1973.

Martin O'Boyle Sr started this company over fifty years ago on a simple promise: do landscape work the right way, treat people fairly, and keep showing up. His son runs it now, with the same standard.

Our story

A family business, second generation.

We started in 1973 with one truck and a mower route. 50+ years later, we design and install landscapes and hardscape, run a full lawn and garden maintenance business, and stock Brookside Garden Center right on Broad Street.

The business is still family-owned and family-run. Most of our work is in Essex County & Northern New Jersey — estates, homes, and commercial properties where ownership wants a partner, not a vendor.
